Cherry Puddin' is a pinch of the blues, a dash of garage, a sprinkle of psychedelic, and a whole lot of Rock and Roll. Cherry Puddin' was first whipped up in late 2021 after former band mates Brian and Tim invited Sam to join their late night jam sessions. Influenced by bands such as ? and The Mysterians, The Ramones, and Greatful Dead, this power trio keeps their audiences on their toes by mixing up classic songs with trance invoking jams. The lineup consists of Tim on vocals and bass, Brian on guitar, and Sam on drums.
